University is a time of experimentation and self-discovery, and most people solidify their values during their college years.\r\n&amp;nbsp;\r\nI want to help clean up the oceans! Perhaps I&amp;rsquo;ll choose an environmental policy major.\r\n&amp;nbsp;\r\nPick a major based on interests\r\n&amp;nbsp;\r\nYour interests are a fine way of determining which major to choose in college. Passions are areas of deep interest, sure, but they also incorporate your values and abilities into something that becomes a burning, lifelong desire.\r\n&amp;nbsp;\r\nFollowing your passions, whatever they may be, is one of the best ways to choose a major, and it generally has the least second-guessing later on.
